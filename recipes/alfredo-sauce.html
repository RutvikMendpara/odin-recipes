Alfredo Sauce

Description

TRich and creamy! I have found that Parmesan cheese doesn't melt well, and often substitute Gruyere cheese.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat.
  2. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly, heating through.
  4. Stir in parsley and serve.
